My name is Steve Spisak, and I am running to serve you as your next teacher bargaining unit
president, here in Halton.

I have a wealth of experience of over 20 years attending local and provincial meetings,
being part of exec, being a branch president, and extensive committee work.

increase, which has been floated around. I want to personally meet all the new hires as
president, and be present at some PA days and staff meetings. I want to improve
communication between the president and the members and be an advocate for the members.
